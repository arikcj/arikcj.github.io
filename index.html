I am a Research Scientist and assistant professor at Shenzhen University. Before that I received B.Sc. degree in Taizhou University, M.Sc. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Zhejiang University, Ph.D degree in Computer Science from National University of Singapore, and postdoctoral work in MIT Singapore Research Center.My research interests are machine learning, multi-agent system, robotics, intelligent transportation system and intelligent healthcare system.
